# Blinkit-PowerBI-Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

⚡ **Blinkit Analytics: Sales, Inventory & Customer Intelligence Dashboard**

A dynamic, multi-section Power BI report built to monitor Blinkit's business performance across sales, inventory health, marketing efficiency, and customer retention — all in one view.

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Blinkit Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI report designed to give business stakeholders a real-time view of key operational metrics. It covers four core business areas — Sales Performance, Inventory Management, Marketing Analytics, and Customer Behavior — enabling data-driven decisions across category management, stock planning, campaign optimization, and customer retention strategy.

This tool is intended for use by operations managers, category analysts, marketing teams, and business leads who need a single source of truth for Blinkit's performance metrics.

## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for report creation and publishing.
- 📁 **Power Query** – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping raw data into analysis-ready tables.
- 🧮 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures including Current Year Sales, YoY comparisons, stock percentage calculations, and customer segmentation metrics.
- 📝 **Data Modeling** – Relationships established across sales, inventory, customer, and marketing tables to enable cross-filtering and drill-down.
- 📁 **File Format** – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

## 4. Data Source

**Source:** Blinkit internal sales and operations dataset.

Data covering product-level sales transactions, inventory stock records, customer acquisition and retention logs, and marketing campaign performance — structured across multiple relational tables spanning current and previous year periods.

## 5. Features / Highlights

**Business Problem**

Blinkit operates across multiple product categories with high delivery velocity, making it difficult for business teams to track sales trends, monitor inventory health, and measure marketing ROI simultaneously without a unified analytics layer.

**Key questions the dashboard answers:**
- How do current year sales compare to the previous year across categories and products?
- Which products are driving the most revenue (Amul Butter, Banana, Britannia Bread, Aashirvaad Atta)?
- What is the current stock health — available vs. damaged inventory?
- How effectively are marketing campaigns converting impressions to revenue?
- What is the ratio of new vs. retained vs. lost customers?

## 6. Business Impact & Insights

- **Inventory Optimization:** 95% available stock rate indicates strong supply chain health; the 0.50 damaged stock rate flags areas needing quality control attention.
- **Marketing Efficiency:** With 7M impressions converting to 544K conversions and 2.97M revenue, the conversion funnel can be benchmarked and optimized per campaign.
- **Top Product Focus:** Amul Butter leads current year sales — signals strong demand for dairy staples; useful for category planning and promotional prioritization.
- **Customer Retention Risk:** 19K lost customers tracked against 25K total signals a retention challenge worth addressing through loyalty or re-engagement campaigns.
- **Category Mix:** Instant & Frozen Food and Pet Care dominate the sales category mix, guiding assortment and shelf-space strategy.
